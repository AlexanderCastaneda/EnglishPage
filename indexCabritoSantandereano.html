Ingredientes

For her preparation, are needs approximately five kilos of goat meat, some vinegar, a spoonful of cumin,
some beer, some oregano, a spoonful of black pimiento, salt, one onion, some garlic, and a bunch of coriander.


How to prepare?

First, bring goat meat and put a large aluminum pan. Before you can pour vinegar on the goat meat.
Can use your hands to mix the goat meat and vinegar. After, rinse with cool water and dry with absorbent paper.
In a wooden mortar, crush garlic, salt, cumin, oregano, and the black pimiento.
Can warn up a furnace to 180 grades Celsius.
In a big pun, warm up some oil and seal goat meat on all sides until it is golden.
Now you have to into goat meat in a furnace and pour beer over it. So, let's now cover the goat meat with aluminum paper for a few minutes.
Now, congratulations your goat meat is ready.
