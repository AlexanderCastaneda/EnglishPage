Ingredients

For her preparation, are needs approximately carrots, salt, three spoonfuls of vinegar, many native potatoes, many cow trips,
garbage, too needs some corn, garlic, one unit of onion, tomatoes, one aguacate, some beef, and one minced beef hand.


How to prepare?

Step 1 For a start, you can wash cow trips and beef hands very well, letting them soak in vinegar with water for some 15 minutes.
After, you can wash it again with water and put it to cook in a pan with some salt.
Step 2 meantime we can grind mead in pieces and add it in a pressure cooker together with cow trips and beef hand. Once one begins
to beep, we can left cook until approximately 20 minutes.
Step 3
Then the beef is cooked, you can grind groceries, how carrots, native potatoes, black potatoes.
Too can you, cook beans and chickpeas separately using some salt.
Step 4
When beef is cooked, use a large pan, and mix all precooked ingredients you can also add hot water to cover all ingredients and cook until reaches boiling point. At this moment you can add black potatoes and native potatoes.
Step 5
Finally, you can add grind coriander, left to soup cook a slow fire for 15 minutes more.
And ready, you can serve your Mute Santandereano and enjoy of this delicious traditional dish.
